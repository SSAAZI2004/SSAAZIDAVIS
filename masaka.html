about us

Featuring free WiFi, Gardens, Masaka Social Centre offers accommodation in Masaka. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free private parking is available on site.
Some units have a seating area for your convenience. Bathrooms are fitted with a shower and extras include slippers and free toiletries. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is featured.
The Masaka Social Centre has an onsite restaurant service breakfast, lunch and dinner. They serve an a la carte menu and room service is also available. The carteen is a relaxed area for sipping cocktails.
There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Shuttle services and tours can be arrange at the 24-hour front desk.
